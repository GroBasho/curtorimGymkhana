Curtorim

Gymkhana

Curtorim Gymkhana established in 1964, one of Goa's oldest and illustrious village clubs, that has gone on to produce some of the most imperious footballers at the national and international level . To create a village level brotherly atmosphere as well as to create a relation with other villages we organise every year interward and intervillage tournaments and many kind of matches and competition. Our villagers always rendered a helping hand and encouragement. In social field we organise every year our traditional kermes on 18th December the feast of Our Lady of Guadalupe ( Banana feast) and drama(Tiart) during Carnival.
