Curtorim Villagers Cup 2023

1st May, 2023

Rossman Cruz Nagoa Verna lift the Curtorim villagers cup 2023 by defeating Navelim sporting by 2-1 finals played at DSYA ground Curtorim.

Following prizes were awarded:
First scorer : Beneston Barreto ( Rossman Cruz)
Best Goalkeeper : Aston Braganza ( Rossman Cruz)
Best defender : Noel Dsilva (Rossman Cruz)
Best midfielder : Desmond perrera ( Rossman Cruz )
Best player of the tournament: Clinton Niasso ( Navelim sporting)
Man of the match: Beneston Barreto ( Rossman Cruz)
Best Forward : Beneston Barreto ( Rossman Cruz)
Best Discipline team : CAC Cansaulim
On this occasion Mr Alexio Reginaldo lourenco MLA was the chief guest and facilitate three sports personalties of Curtorim, Two represented Goa for Santosh trophy. Jovel Martin & Nixon Castanha and Maria Rebello( Ex captain women's team India & Referee Assessor for Olympic qualifiers) for doing proud for the constituency in football and in name of his father gave away cash award and gave away the individual prizes in the presence of Michelle Rebello( Zp Curtorim), Rui Menezes, Armando colaco, Roque Mascarenhas, Sir Gromiko fernandes, Santan Mascarenhas, Fr lourdin Pereira, John D costa ,Shane Rebello and club officials

Unveiling a New Era: The Inauguration of Curtorim Gymkhana's Club House

4th March, 2023

It is with great pride and honour, I present to you, my Club Members, well-wishers, and fellow Villagers, the website of Curtorim Gymkhana. This was possible only due to the untiring efforts of my Executive Committee who gave their time and thoughts to get this done. Curtorim Gymkhana will henceforth be active on social media of which I am proud of. I am also proud to announce that Curtorim Gymkhana will cater to sportspersons from various fields starting with Chess. I humbly thank my fellow Members of Curtorim Gymkhana for entrusting me with the role of the President and ensure that I will put Curtorim Gymkhana in the world map from here on with positive results and further encourage our youngsters in the field of sports. As I conclude, I would like to dedicate my efforts to my late father Mr. Vincent Fernandes who had been my guide throughout and was one of the hard core members of Curtorim Gymkhana. Thank you. God bless.

Victory Against St. Anthony Assolda

6th March, 2023

Curtorim Gymkhana beats St. Anthony Assolda 1-0 at Cuncolim ground with Joy Hilario's match-winning goal. Joy Hilario from Curtorim Gymkhana is awarded the man of the match.
